Select Royal Maids Agency is your trusted partner providing your family with domestic staffing and highly experienced in placement for housekeeping services. We are here to take the stress off you.

Our housekeeping approach is vast and encompassing depending on the needs of your family or home. We invest in training our housekeepers to expertly handle common household chores that include cleaning bathrooms, scrubbing floors, vacuuming, and mopping.

Why Choose Us

Full Background Check

We conduct a rigorous and comprehensive background check to assess their family history, previous employment, religious background and residential history.

Guarantor Backing

All our candidates must provide 2 guarantors, who are well standing members of the community, and who must sign to be vicariously liable.

Medically Tested

Before placing a candidate in your household, we test them for pregnancy, HIV, hepatitis B and tuberculosis using a regulated medical laboratory.

Home Verification

We visit and verify the residential addresses of both the candidate and their guarantors.

Training & Development

All our candidates are given an orientation before their employment starts, and are provided with quarterly skills acquisition training each year.

Risk Assessment

All our candidates are assessed on various areas of potential threats they may pose to you and your family, including stealing, promiscuity, excessive phone usage etc.

Replacement

If at any time you are unhappy with the conduct or performance of your househelp, we can offer you an immediate replacement.

Complaints & Discipline

If your househelp misbehaves, you can report it to us and we will take swift action to punish them in accordance with our strict disciplinary procedures.

Police Station Registration

We work hand in hand with the police to counter sign all our documents and prosecute any candidate that commits a crime whilst in your employment.
